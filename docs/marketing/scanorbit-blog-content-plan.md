# ScanOrbit — Blog Content Plan & SEO Calendar

## Publishing Cadence

**1 article per week.** As a solo founder, this is sustainable and sufficient to build SEO momentum. Publish on Tuesday or Wednesday mornings (best for B2B engagement). Each article should be 1,200–2,000 words with clear structure, actionable content, and a natural CTA to ScanOrbit.

Total plan: **24 articles over 6 months**, organized in 3 phases.

## On-Page SEO Checklist (Apply to Every Article)

1. Target keyword in title (H1), URL slug, meta description, and first 100 words
2. 2–4 H2 subheadings containing keyword variations
3. Internal links to 2–3 other ScanOrbit blog posts (build cluster)
4. One external link to an authoritative source (AWS docs, GDPR text, security research)
5. Meta description under 155 characters with clear value proposition
6. URL structure: scanorbit.cloud/blog/[keyword-slug]
7. Alt text on all images with keyword context
8. Schema markup: Article type with author (Maksim), datePublished, dateModified
9. CTA in the article body (not just at the end) — after demonstrating the manual pain, offer ScanOrbit as the automated solution
10. Estimated reading time displayed on the page

## Distribution Plan Per Article

Each article should be distributed through these channels on publish day and the following week:

**Day of publish:**
- Post to personal Twitter/X with a hook (not just the title — lead with the insight or stat)
- Share in 1–2 relevant subreddits (r/aws, r/devops, r/sysadmin, r/netsec depending on topic) with genuine value — don't just drop a link

**Day after:**
- Cross-post key insight as a LinkedIn post or Twitter/X thread
- Add to any relevant Hacker News thread if timing works (don't force it)

**Within the week:**
- Repurpose core insight into a newsletter segment for your subscriber list
- Add internal links from 2–3 existing articles back to the new one
